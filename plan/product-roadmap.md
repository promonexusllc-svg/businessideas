- [ ] Write compelling Etsy listings with SEO keywords

- [ ] Create bundle packages (e.g., "Small Business Starter Kit" = Invoice + Budget + Expense Report)
- [ ] Add 5 more individual templates
- [ ] Launch on both Etsy + Gumroad
- [ ] Set up Pinterest account for free marketing
- [ ] Create product preview videos

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 2-3)
- [ ] Reach 15+ individual products
- [ ] Create 3-5 bundles at premium pricing
- [ ] Start collecting customer feedback
- [ ] Launch "Custom Template" service as premium offering

### Phase 4: Automate & Grow (Month 3-6)
- [ ] Analyze which products sell best → double down
- [ ] Create seasonal/trending templates
- [ ] Build automated social media presence
- [ ] Explore adding Notion templates
- [ ] Consider premium membership/subscription model
- [ ] Expand to Shopify or own website

## 🏪 Custom Template Service

**Offering:** "Need something specific? We create custom templates tailored to your exact needs."

**Process:**
1. Customer fills out a request form (what they need, how many sheets, features)
2. We provide a quote within 24 hours
3. Template delivered within 3-5 business days
4. One round of revisions included

**Pricing:** $50–$150 depending on complexity

This differentiates us from every other template shop and creates a high-margin revenue stream.
